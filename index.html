Introduction

The Word Counter is a free online tool designed to help writers, students, bloggers, and professionals quickly calculate word and character counts. Whether you’re drafting an essay, social media post, or article, this tool provides instant insights.

What is Word Counter Tool?

A Word Counter Tool measures the number of words, characters, and sentences in your text. It also estimates the reading time based on average reading speed (200 words per minute). This helps you maintain ideal content length for SEO, readability, or assignments.

How to Use?

  • Type or paste your text in the box above.
  • Results update automatically as you type.
  • Check word count, character count, and reading time instantly.
  • Use it to optimize blog posts, essays, or professional documents.

Conclusion

The Word Counter tool saves time and helps ensure your content meets specific requirements. Simple, fast, and accurate — perfect for anyone who writes online or offline.
